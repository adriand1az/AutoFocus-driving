About us

Focus Driving School has been serving the community for over 30 years, formerly known as Rutland Driving School run by Denzil Millar (My Father). We are committed to excellence. Our students are our greatest asset. We have an excellent record taking new drivers and turning them into excellent drivers and taking bad habits and turning them into good habits. Or maybe you just need a brush up before you get back on the road.

It doesn't matter what condition you come to us in. Nervous, scared, confused... By the time we are finished with you, you will be cool, calm, confident and road test ready. Focus Driving School has the nerve, the patience and the skill to teach you how to drive. Our instructors are licensed by the DMV and are professionally trained, patient and knowledgeable. I hope you enjoy your experience.
